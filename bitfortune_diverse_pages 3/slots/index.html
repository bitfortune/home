Volatility and RTP

Volatility and RTP are often misunderstood, so they need a dedicated explanation on any serious slot page. RTP is a long-term theoretical figure, not a promise for one player or one session. Volatility describes how wins may be distributed, with some games paying smaller amounts more often and others having longer gaps between feature moments. A Bitfortune player should use these numbers as context rather than as a guarantee. It is also important to remember that a high RTP does not remove risk, and a volatile game can still drain a budget quickly if stakes are too large. The safest approach is to set the stake size before playing and keep it consistent.
